These are the formulas used to calculate de MOD and EAD. Note that if you slide the depth range you are then calculating the best mix for the choosen depth.

  • MOD (Maximun Operational Depth): 10 x ((ppO2/FO2)-1)
  • EAD (Equivalent Air Depth) : (((1-eanx)*(mod+10))/0.79)-10
  • Best Mix : ppo2/((depth/10)+1)
Eanx stands for 'Enriched Air NitroX'
